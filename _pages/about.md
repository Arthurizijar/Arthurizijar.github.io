I visited <a href="https://mbzuai.ac.ae/">MBZUAI</a> from September 2024 to August 2025 under the guidance of Prof. <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/qsun">Qiang Sun</a>, and have also worked with the Embedding Team at <a href="https://qwenlm.github.io/">Qwen</a> in Beijing.</p>
